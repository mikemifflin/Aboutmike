Late Night Discussion

Mike Mifflin

My understanding of what comes to be,

is only limiting my own reality,

define this life, which can twist and turn,

only to burn whats left to learn.

Unconditioned years of total blindness

focused on our mere sight of silence.

Darkness draped over our eyes wide shut,

deceiving and repeating lies lied shut.

Unlearn what you have learned in order to escape,

this prison that is created through our own dreamscape.

Break through the walls of our own demise,

and shine with divine inspirited minds.

But first we all must walk a path of that is unclear,

dampened by our egos and disguised with fear.

Almost destroying our well being of love,

damaging us until we plead from above.

Little do we know, above is within,

without is our hell which we all claim to live in.

Designed and defined by our thoughts of self,

the life that we create is like nothing else.
